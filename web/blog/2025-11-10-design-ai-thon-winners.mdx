We have to admit - judging was *tough*. We asked you to reimagine Wasp's website, and you absolutely delivered. Some of you went full Figma wizard mode, others shipped actual working code, and one person even brought in a full vibe-coded OS (more on that later üëÄ).

When we said "no rules," Zeke took it to heart and delivered a complete overhaul of Wasp's visual identity. We're talking full redesign - new layout, refreshed copy, the works. But what really made this submission stand out wasn't just the end result though it looked great, it was the *journey*.

Zeke didn't just drop a Figma file and call it a day. He documented his entire thought process, breaking down every design decision, every copy change, and the reasoning behind it all. He even created 6 different drawings of boi!

Remember when we said we wanted something you don't see every day? Etienne delivered exactly that. Imagine if Wasp's website was designed like a developer's notebook, complete with that hand-drawn, sketch-style aesthetic.

It's playful, it's memorable, and it's *fun*. In a world where most dev tool websites look like they came from the same template, Etienne's design stands out by leaning into personality over polish. The notebook-style approach feels authentic and creative - like you're peeking into someone's actual design process.

This is the kind of out-of-the-box thinking we were hoping to see. It's a design that makes you smile, and honestly, that's exactly the vibe Wasp is all about (next to building web apps).

## Most Technically Impressive üíª

**Winner: [Adam (bO-05)](https://github.com/bO-05)**

**See it in action**: [link](https://wasp-clone-1.vercel.app)

<video width="100%" controls>
  <source src="/img/design-ai-thon-winners/adam-entry.mp4" type="video/mp4" />

Remember that vibe-coded OS we mentioned earlier? Yeah, this is it. Adam didn't just redesign Wasp's website - he built an *entire interactive desktop OS playground* where you can explore Wasp like you're using an actual operating system.

We're talking a fully functional virtual desktop complete with file management, a working terminal, notes app, and even an AI-powered chat assistant. Want to save your work? There's optional cloud sync with authentication. Adam literally shipped a whole OS experience for a website redesign competition.

The technical execution here is wild, but what really sealed the deal was how it all comes together with this futuristic, high-vibe aesthetic. It's not just technically impressive - it's *cool*. You could spend 20 minutes just playing around with it, which is exactly what I did.
