## [CutCue](https://cutcue.io/) — the AI editing intern for streamers, podcasters, and editors

<ImgWithCaption
    framed
    alt="CutCue landing page — 'AI-powered tool that analyses your audio and video, generating timestamped markers'"
    source="img/made-with-wasp-vol2/app-cutcue.webp"
/>

**Try it out:** [cutcue.io](https://cutcue.io/)  ·  **Pricing:** €29–€189/mo

Anyone who's sat through a six-hour Twitch VOD trying to find the one good moment knows exactly the pain CutCue is solving. Drop in a recording and it spits out timestamped markers for highlights, chapters, brand-mention keywords, demonetization risks, and chat peaks — without you ever scrubbing through it.

The clever part is that the markers don't sit in some dashboard you have to copy-paste from. CutCue exports straight into Premiere Pro, DaVinci Resolve, Vegas, Audacity, and Reaper, so they land in the editor exactly where your timeline expects them. It's "the AI part of the edit, without leaving the edit."

Three plans, €29 to €189/mo, with creator-friendly credit math (one credit ≈ one minute of audio). The funniest detail: the maker isn't an editor themselves — they just watched enough editors burn hours scrubbing VODs to decide that part shouldn't be human work. The kind of niche, prosumer SaaS Wasp is well suited for: real pain, clear willingness to pay.

## [Brick](https://onbrick.co.uk/) — a website builder for UK property operators

<ImgWithCaption
    framed
    alt="Brick landing page — 'Launch your property website in five minutes' for UK property operators"
    source="img/made-with-wasp-vol2/app-brick.webp"
/>

**Try it out:** [onbrick.co.uk](https://onbrick.co.uk/)  ·  **Pricing:** £45–£119/mo

The kind of vertical SaaS that quietly prints money. Brick is a website builder aimed squarely at UK property operators — landlords, serviced accommodation hosts, HMO managers, rent-to-rent businesses, deal sourcers. Five minutes from sign-up to a live, professional-looking property site, with AI-generated copy, niche templates per property type, a built-in lead-capture chatbot, and all the GDPR/SSL/compliance plumbing handled for you.

It's the answer to a very specific question: *"I'm a UK property operator, I need credibility online, I don't want to spend three grand on an agency or two weekends on Webflow."* That focus shows in every part of the product — there's nothing generic about it. Sixty-plus property businesses are running on Brick today, with 96% retention month over month, which is the kind of number you only get when your niche actually loves you.

We love this one because it makes a point: Wasp isn't only for generic dashboards and developer tools. It can carry a real, domain-deep, non-technical-audience SaaS in a vertical we know nothing about — and that's exactly how it should be.

## [ChatBuster](https://chatbuster.com/) — the AI chat that closes the sale, live on Shopify and WordPress

<ImgWithCaption
    framed
    alt="ChatBuster landing page — 'The chat that closes the sale. AI trained on your store, answering questions 24/7'"
    source="img/made-with-wasp-vol2/app-chatbuster.webp"
/>

**Try it out:** [chatbuster.com](https://chatbuster.com/)  ·  **Pricing:** from $40/mo  ·  **Where:** Shopify App Store + WordPress.org

This is the distribution flex of the bunch. ChatBuster is an AI chat widget for Shopify and WooCommerce stores — trained on your catalog, theme, and policies, answering buyer questions 24/7 ("do you ship to Canada?", "what's the return policy?") so visitors don't bounce mid-purchase.

The part we keep coming back to: it lives in **two** of the largest e-commerce ecosystems in the world. You install it from the Shopify App Store. You install it from WordPress.org (the plugin went live there a few weeks ago, in May 2026). Standing up a Wasp app and slotting it into those plugin marketplaces is a quietly big deal — it puts the "real fullstack framework" promise to a test most frameworks never actually face.

Under the hood there's more than reactive Q&A: live catalog sync (SKUs, prices, stock), proactive prompts you can trigger on scroll or schedule, and order tracking via the store's API. "No developer needed, set up in minutes" is the pitch — and it lands exactly that way for the merchant.

## [PlanRelief](https://planrelief.io/) — AI lesson plans for daycare and early-years educators

<ImgWithCaption
    framed
    alt="PlanRelief landing page — 'Generate lesson plans. Instantly.' AI-powered lesson plans for educators of ages 0–12"
    source="img/made-with-wasp-vol2/app-planrelief.webp"
/>

**Try it out:** [planrelief.io](https://planrelief.io/)  ·  **For:** educators of ages 0–12

The mission one. PlanRelief generates lesson plans for educators working with kids ages 0 through 12 — daycare, kindergarten, early primary. The pitch line is exactly what you'd want it to be: *"paperwork done in minutes, not hours."*

You record an observation ("Mia experimented with mixing colours"), pick a theme, and PlanRelief generates the full plan — learning objectives, materials list, assessment strategies, the whole template, in a format you can actually hand to a supervisor. Free to try, no credit card required.

This one quietly answers a different question entirely: *"is Wasp only for developers building things for other developers?"* Apparently not. It's also, by a comfortable margin, the most wholesome app on this list.
