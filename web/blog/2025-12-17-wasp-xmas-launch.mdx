You're gonna love this one. We keep hearing from so many developers what a great fit Claude Code combined with Wasp is, so we decided to take things one step further - introducing official **Claude Code plugin for Wasp**!

If you haven't heard of Claude Code plugins yet - it's a way to extend Claude with custom functionality. Think of it as giving Claude superpowers for specific frameworks and workflows.

Here's what it does:

- **Knows Wasp inside-out** - Plugs in Wasp best practices and points Claude to the right documentation (even accounting for your Wasp version!)
- **Guides you through common tasks** - Adding auth, email sending, deployment, and more
- **Embedded workflows** - Common patterns and advice baked right in, so Claude can help you build the "Wasp way"

It's like having a Wasp expert sitting next to you while you code. AI-assisted development just got a whole lot better for Wasp developers! ğŸ¤–

[Polar](https://polar.sh) is now the hottest way to add billing to your SaaS. Think of it like Wasp, but for payment systems - it's extremely fast to add and does a ton of heavy lifting for you (subscriptions, usage-based billing, and MoR). It's also 100% open-source, so no wonder we like it!

We're integrating Polar directly into [Open SaaS](https://opensaas.sh/), making it even simpler to start accepting payments and building your SaaS business.
